QUIZOMANIA
(Battle of the erudites)


Introduction

Name of event: Quizomania
Date of event: 2/10/21
Platform for event: Google meet
Number of attendees: 50





The Event

Quizomania an intra college quiz competition was organized by the DSAII Club on the occasion of Gandhi Jayanti to encourage the students widen their knowledge in the vast field of python and problem solving. It was an individual event with three rounds. The questions were cleverley framed and perfectly covered every topics that comes under the set of python and problem solving.

Due to the pandemic the quiz was conducted online but it still didn't stop the members from hosting such a successful and interactive event.50 students of second year represented their respective branches which proved that having a knowledge of AI is not limited to the computer science branch.

The preliminary round started at 11am and was open for 20 mins. students were selected for the second round. The second round started at 11:30 am and was open for 20 mins. 6 students qualified for the final round. Top two were awarded as winners. The first prize winner was Prathamesh Patil of second year and second prize winner was Subham Kardel of second year . Overall, the Quiz competition was a huge success.

The team of DSAII feels immense pride in organising an event that can only be considered successful, and would also like to convey our thanks to the principal, the HOD Professor Santosh Chobe, Traning and Placement professor Dheeraj Agarwal and professor Subhangi Suryawanshi for their timely advice and co-operation on the launching of the club.
